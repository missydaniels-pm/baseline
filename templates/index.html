Welcome to Baseline!

Baseline helps you track your symptoms, run structured experiments, and build real evidence for your health decisions.

Instead of guessing what works, you'll have data — your own personal record of what you've tried and what the results were.

This quick tour will show you the key features.

Your Dashboard

The dashboard is your home base. As you log, you'll see:

  • Symptom cards — your average scores this month vs. your baseline
  • Trend charts — episode frequency and symptom severity over 12 weeks
  • Rescue effectiveness — which rescue options work best for you

Charts appear after about two weeks of logging.

The Daily Check-in

If you've enabled AI logging, the check-in is the easiest way to log. Just describe your day in plain language:

"Rough morning, headache around a 6, took my Sumatriptan at 10am, feeling better by noon."

Baseline extracts the data and confirms what it logged. If it gets something wrong, just correct it in the next message.

Experiments

This is what makes Baseline different. When you start a new protocol, track it as an experiment:

  • Define your hypothesis and timeframe
  • Keep logging as normal during the experiment
  • When it's time, assess the outcome with your actual data

Over time, you build an evidence base — a real record of what you've tried and what worked.

Get started

Here are a few things you can do right now:

  • Log your first episode — record a recent symptom event
  • Try the daily check-in — describe your day and let AI handle the rest
  • Add a protocol — track a medication or supplement you're currently taking

You can replay this tour anytime from the Help page.
