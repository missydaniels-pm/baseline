Baseline

Privacy Policy

Effective March 6, 2026

Baseline is a personal health tracking application. We take the privacy of your health data seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights — including those under the Washington My Health My Data Act.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address and a hashed password. We do not collect your real name unless you choose to provide it.
  • Health data you enter: episodes (symptom scores, severity, duration, notes), protocols, experiments, rescue medications, and daily compliance logs.
  • AI check-in messages: if you opt in to AI logging, the text you type on the Check-in page is sent to Anthropic's API to generate structured health records. These messages are stored in your account history.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the Baseline service — storing, displaying, and analyzing your health tracking data.
  • To generate dashboard insights (charts, trends, experiment assessments) from your own data.
  • If AI logging is enabled, to send your check-in text to Anthropic's Claude API for natural language processing. Anthropic's data usage policy applies to that interaction.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or any instrumentation that sends your health content to third parties without your explicit consent.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.
  • We do not track you across other websites or apps.

4. Third-Party Services

Anthropic API (opt-in only): when you enable AI logging in Settings, your check-in messages are sent to Anthropic's API. Anthropic's privacy policy governs their handling of that data. You can disable AI logging at any time in Settings.

Railway: the application is hosted on Railway. Railway's infrastructure may process standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps). See Railway's privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway's infrastructure.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and are never stored in plain text.
  • Access to the application requires an invite code — there is no public registration.
  • All connections use HTTPS encryption in transit.

6. Your Rights

Under the Washington My Health My Data Act and our commitment to your privacy, you have the right to:

  • Access: view all health data stored in your account at any time through the app.
  • Delete: permanently delete your account and all associated health data from Settings. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
  • Withdraw consent: disable AI logging at any time in Settings to stop sending data to Anthropic's API.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all data — episodes, protocols, experiments, compliance logs, check-in history, and symptom records — is permanently removed from our database.

8. Children

Baseline is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the application. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at baselinehealthapp@gmail.com.
